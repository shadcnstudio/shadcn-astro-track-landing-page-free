Learn how to create and maintain design systems that empower teams to build consistent, scalable products efficiently.

## What is a Design System?

A design system is a collection of reusable components, guidelines, and standards that help teams build consistent digital products.

### Core Elements

- Component library
- Design tokens
- Documentation
- Usage guidelines

## Benefits of Design Systems

### Consistency

Ensures a unified look and feel across all products and platforms.

### Efficiency

Speeds up development by providing ready-to-use components and patterns.

### Scalability

Makes it easier to scale design and development efforts as teams and products grow.

## Building a Design System

### Start Small

Begin with the most commonly used components and expand over time.

### Collaborate Across Teams

Involve designers, developers, and stakeholders to ensure adoption and relevance.

### Document Everything

Clear documentation is key for onboarding and consistent usage.

## Maintaining and Evolving

- Regularly review and update components
- Gather feedback from users
- Adapt to new technologies and trends

## Conclusion

A well-built design system is a powerful tool for scaling products and teams efficiently in 2026 and beyond.
